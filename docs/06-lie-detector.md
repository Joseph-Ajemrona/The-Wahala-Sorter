1. **Loading starting chores**: When the app opens, it checks the secret storage drawer for chores you saved; if the drawer is empty, it puts three starter Lagos hustle chores on the board.

2. **Adding a new chore**: When you add a chore, the app cleans up the text you typed, stamps a unique ID tag and a current clock time on it, and places it at the very top of the box you chose.

3. **Clearing the boxes**: After a chore is successfully added, the typing box goes completely blank and the dropdown menu resets back to the first box ("Now").

4. **Switching themes**: The app checks the secret storage drawer to see if you like the dark theme or light theme; if it cannot find anything, it asks your computer what color style it is currently using, and then colors the page to match.

5. **Dragging chores**: When you grab a chore card to move it, the app remembers its ID tag. When you drop it on a column, the app updates its column name to match and forgets you are holding it.
